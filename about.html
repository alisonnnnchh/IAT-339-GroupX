Our Story

Vintage Maze is a new vintage store located in the Lower Mainland Vancouver Area, where you can browse the latest in vintage clothing, toys, home decoration items, accessories, and more. We aim to be able to give our customers the best quality vintage merchandise that will look great for your daily life, such as in your home or car.

Opened in 2019, Vintage Maze was started by Chinese immigrants to Canada who had a love for the vintage clothing styles of the past, but struggled to find quality vintage places that were not labelled with the same connotations as a thrift store. The store aims to fill a gap by providing only high quality merchandise.

The Maze's Meaning

The maze imagery hopes to evoke thoughts of being "lost in the past ". We hope the vintage items provided help our customers to take a trip down their memory lanes.

The Items Collected

To evoke and remind our customers of their good childhood memories of the past, Vintage Maze has collections of retro furnishings, second-hand antique accessories, toys, clothes and hand-made designs around the world.

Collections We Have

We house a colletion of items you can decorate you and your house with: clothing, accessories, home decor, and toys. In shopping at Vintage Maze, we can help vintage items become a small part of all aspects of your life.

About the Owner

The owner, Amy Li, is a 2010 graduate of Simon Fraser University with a BA in History and Asian Studies.

She attributes her love for vintage fashion to her parents, who decorated her childhood home with what became known as vintage styles in the modern day. As these vintage items evokes warm childhood memories for her, she hopes to do the same thing for others. She gains inspiration by browsing pictures in fashion magazines and blogs as she grew up.

In her spare time, she enjoys window shopping, travelling, and playing arcade crane games.
