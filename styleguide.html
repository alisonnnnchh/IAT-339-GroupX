Vintage Maze Style Guide

Brand Defintion:

Vintage Maze collects the parts and pieces from everyone's memories. To evoke the good childhood, shared memories, Vintage Maze has collections of retro furnishings, second-hand antique accessories, toys, clothes and hand-made designs around the world.
